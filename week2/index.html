About Me

My name is Jonelle Harris and I am 22 years old. Before I got into tech, I initially pursued my previous "passion" in the arts by going to an art school. When the pandemic took the world by storm and everywhere was locked down, I decided to explore my other interest in computers and other technology and further develop my skills. I took a class learning skills and techniques within the IT industry and while I was in the class I found that the tech field is where I'm most passionate in. I decided afterwards that I wanted to continue to learn more about computers and coding and I took another class learning how to code. After the class, I felt that I wanted to further my knowledge in coding as I ultimately decided to pursue a career in software development. I am currently taking a bootcamp course in coding at the University of Toronto as I believe that with their knowledgeable staff and resources, they will help me reach my goals and aid me to my path to success.
